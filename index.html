About Me

My name is Joel Roble. I am an aspiring web developer and filmmaker who enrolled in SPaCE to get a certificate
that will help me find a job. I chose the Diploma Program In UI/UX For Web Design and Development because I have
some experience with coding during high school and because of the lucrative opportunities found in tech-based
careers like Web Development.

My hobbies/interests include reading books, watching films, playing video games, learning history and playing chess.
I developed a passion for film because of a group project in Grade 8 English, where I made a short film with my
classmates. Despite the challenges we faced during the project, I managed to transform the footage we captured over
the course of 2-3 weeks into a watchable short film with the power of audiovisual editing.
